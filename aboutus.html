Art House Cafe is a destination discovery website that helps travellers find the right destinations according to their preferences and help them plan their holidays in a hassle free manner. At Art House Cafe, we aim to make discovering your next holiday destination as much of an eye-opener as your vacation itself. We help you break out of the monotony of the same vacation spots you have known for years and open in front of you, a world of possibilities. With our interactive map interface and powerful filters, you can plan your vacation like never before.
